WHAT IS THIS

Hello my dudes, I'm Daria. If you're here on this website' it probably means that a) I pestered you long enough for you to give up and go "Ugh okay I'll give it a look just stop talking" and it that case thank you for this heart-warming display of friendship, or b) that you arrived here by accident, in which case I would suggest to run before it's too late and reexaminate your life.

You might have noticed the total absence of good taste in the design of this place. While I really want to make the excuse that I recently became pretty much obsessed by the V A P O R W A V E A E S T H E T I C S, the truth is actually much simpler : I am a bad designer and my tastes are god awful. I mean I can always say that "you guys don't GET me". I'm an artist after all *insert multiple crying-while-laughing emojis*.

Anyways, what you will find here is one of my attempts at making music, and making a website highlighting said music. One might say that an over the top / magenta-themed / silly exterior isn't the best way to capture the depressing and borderline suicidal mood of my MuSiCaL sOuNdScApEs. to this I would answer : "are you dense you butt-nugget, that's kind of the point".

In any case if you like what you see/hear, please leave a like, subscribe and donate to my patreon. Oh wait I don't have a facebook page. Or a Youtube Channel. Or a Patreon. What am I doing with my life again ?

And most importantly, don't forget : "Love To Hate yourself" and "If you ever feel any emotion, of any kind, or are seeking happiness instead of crippling depression, you're doing something wrong."
